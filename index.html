Four simple phases. Everything is handled by us. You show up, and we build together.

4
01
Access & Setup
Day 1

We get you connected. This means access to your Meta account and getting you plugged into our tech stack so leads flow straight to you the moment they hit.

Meta Account Access
We connect to your Meta Business Manager so our ads team can build and run campaigns on your behalf.
CRM Onboarding
Your dedicated CRM account gets configured. Every lead generated by your ads lands here automatically, with follow-up sequences already in place.
02
Training & Offer
Week 1

You get access to our full training library. This walks you through the entire GymGrow process and helps you build the offer and pricing that will convert the leads we send you.

Watch the Training Material
Step-by-step modules covering how the whole system works, what to expect from leads, and how to close them.
Build Your Offer
We help you define the front-end offer that your ads will promote, and lock in pricing that works for your market.
03
First Ads Live
Week 1–2

We don't wait around. As soon as your offer is locked in, we build your first still image ads so we can start generating leads fast while your video content comes together.

Still Image Ad Creation
Our team creates your first batch of static ads using your offer and branding. These go live quickly to get lead flow started immediately.
Campaigns Go Live
Ads are launched. Your CRM is active. Leads start coming in.
04
Scale & Refine
Ongoing

This is where it compounds. You create short video content for ads, join our weekly live calls, and we give you direct feedback on what's working so you can keep improving.

Video Creatives
You record short video ads using our proven frameworks. We brief you on exactly what to say. Our team handles the editing.
Weekly Live Calls
Every week you join our group coaching call. We review results, troubleshoot, and tell you exactly what to do next to keep results moving forward.

Any questions about the process? Reach out to the team before you get started and we'll walk you through anything that needs clarifying.
